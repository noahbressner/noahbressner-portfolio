I am an associate reporter/producer for the Digital Team at the New York Post. My role involves working with both the News and Sports sections. I graduated from Binghamton University in May, where I was the editor in chief of Pipe Dream, the university’s entirely student-run newspaper.


While at Binghamton, I interned at WIVT/WBGH News Channel 34, which acts as both the ABC and NBC affiliate in the area, and I was a DJ at WHRW, Binghamton University’s radio station. I also spent time at the New York State Division of Human Rights and at a hospital in Newark helping a surgeon with data for his research.
