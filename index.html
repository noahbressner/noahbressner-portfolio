I am a senior at Binghamton University and the Managing Editor for Pipe Dream, Binghamton University’s entirely student-run newspaper. It is published twice-a-week, and daily online during the academic year. I have also done freelance work that was published in the Times Union (Albany, N.Y.).

I have interned at WIVT/WBGH NewsChannel 34, which acts as both ABC and NBC’s affiliates in the Binghamton area. I also work at WHRW 90.5 FM, Binghamton University’s radio station, where I engineer a music and two news shows every week, in addition to producing a weekly podcast.
